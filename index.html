Apple Cinnamon Protein Salad

Ingredients

  • Half an Apple (Granny Smith if you want a little sourness to your snack)
  • Half a cup of Greek Yogurt
  • 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  • A pinch of Cinnamon
  • 2 Tablesppons of room temperature Cream Cheese
  • 2 Teaspoons of Brown Sugar

    • Directions

    1.Wash your apple throughly

    2.Cut apple into thin easy to eat slices

    3.Add apples, cinnamon, brown sugar, greek yogurt, cream cheese, and vanilla extract to a jar with an air tight lid

    4.Screw on lid and shake jar aggressivly

    The salad should look like this when you're done.
